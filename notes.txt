STEP 6: ANALYTICS — HOW TO TEST OPENS
--------------------------------------------------------------
⚠️ IMPORTANT: Gmail blocks images by default!
To register an "open", you MUST:
  1. Open the email in Gmail
  2. Click "Display images below" at the top of the email
  3. Refresh the analytics page
✅ Open Rate should update from 0% to 100%

To test click tracking:
  1. Add a link to your email body when creating a campaign
  2. Click that link in the received email
✅ Click rate should update in the analytics dashboard
